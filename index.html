Elastic task remaining

When running time consuming tasks in Elasticsearch, you want to know the time remaining. Elasticsearch does not show a human readable time when it expects to finish. This tool helps you calculate the time left for your Elasticsearch task to complete. It uses the task's completed field and start_time_in_millis and running_time_in_nanos fields to calculate the time remaining. Nothing is stored or sent to a server. Everything is calculated in your browser.
